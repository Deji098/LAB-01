Ayodeji Igiolu - Biography

Beginner Programmer

The Biography

In 2022 Ayodeji Igiolu started his Programming program, but before that he came from Nigeria to Canada at age 11. When he arrived in Canada he first lived in Brampton for 3 years before he moved to Innisfil.

In Innisfil he attended Holy Cross Catholic School and then at the age of 16 he went to St.Peter's Catholic Highschool in Barrie. He graduated in 2020 and due to Covid-19, it was an online graduation.

After Ayodeji graduated in 2020, he went straight into the work force. Ayodeji has a variety of work experience as he worked different jobs to find his passion, he did jobs such as golf course attendant, sales associate, and social worker. Ayodeji felt none of this jobs were for him, so he went on a search for his passion and that landed him in Programming.
